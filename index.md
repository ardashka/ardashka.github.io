I am an SNSF Ambizione Fellow in Theoretical Biophysics at EPFL, Switzerland. My research focuses on computational modelling of biological processes at different scales, from single-cell phenomena to collective behaviour of several cell populations. In particular, I am interested in introducing eco-evolutionary dynamics into the numerical models of active matter.
